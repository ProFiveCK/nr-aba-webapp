How it works

  • Draft batches are fully editable. You can delete them or load them back into the Generator.
  • Submitted batches are read-only. Reviewers see the same information.
  • Rejected batches can be opened in Reader and loaded into the Generator to create a revision.
  • Every action (submit, reject, approve) records who made the change and when.
